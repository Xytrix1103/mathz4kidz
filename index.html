Why Us?

mathz4kidz: How We Teach



Through the UniqGuru platform, students are connected together by mobile gadgets and technology in the classroom, marking a step towards a smart learning environment.

At mathz4kidz, we use methods that are based on learning theories and research as used to develop the textbook series Maths - No Problem! written by our consultant, Dr Yeap Ban Har, and used around the world including in England and the United States of America.

Students are often exposed to different types of fun games that stimulate their thinking processes and spark their enthusiasm. Sometimes, the lesson is inside too!

In every mathz4kidz lesson, children learn and have fun in specially planned group activites - contests, projects, discussions and much more - which encourages them to enjoy their learning journey.

- Our Core Values -

A.C.T.



A

Attitude

Here, a positive and bright attitude is - if not more - as important as a student's progress. Mathz4kidz makes it a point to foster the right attitude in not only their learning but their daily lives too. In a friendly and close-knit learning environment, students feel more confident to put themselves forward.

C

Communication

In all our classes, the act of communicating is greatly encouraged through active student-teacher interaction, which include asking questions, group discussions and friendly inter-student contests. These allow them to develop critical communication skills while making certain they understands the lesson.

T

Thinking

At mathz4kidz, we emphasize on critical thinking in logical manners to solve various problems, in a wide diversity of manners - math questions, puzzles, you name it. This is helpful not only throughout the lessons, but in solving daily life problems as well, embedding STEAM knwoledge along the way too!
