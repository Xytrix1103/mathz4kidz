KenKen Puzzle Championship 2019

Teacher Jimmy employs UniqGuru, a technological platform for classroom use, to engage and excite 80 teachers at Sekolah Pahoa in Indonesia in solving the KenKen puzzle and challenging mathematics problem. KenKen is a tool that helps students develop fluency even as they become stronger in number sense and reasoning ability. mathz4kidz and PMP agreed to collaborate to promote Ken Ken Puzzles in Penang. The aim is to use these puzzles as a learning mathematical tool to master basic math operations, while enhancing computational thinking and problem skills. Penang Math Platform (PMP), one of the STEM centres under Penang STEM Sdn Bhd, is a Penang State government agency chartered with promoting Science-Technology-Engineering-Mathematics (STEM) learning in Penang.
